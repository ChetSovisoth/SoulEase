# Online Therapy Platform

A comprehensive digital platform connecting patients with licensed therapists for secure, convenient online therapy sessions.

---

## Overview

The Online Therapy Platform is a modern telemedicine solution designed to make mental healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient. Our platform facilitates seamless connections between patients seeking therapy and qualified mental health professionals, offering video consultations, secure messaging, and integrated payment processing.

### Mission

To break down barriers to mental healthcare by providing a secure, user-friendly platform that connects people with professional therapists anytime, anywhere.

## How It Works

### For Patients: From Sign-Up to Session

**Step 1: Create Your Account**
- Sign up with your email and password
- Complete your profile with basic information
- Verify your email address

**Step 2: Find Your Therapist**
- Browse therapist profiles and specializations
- Filter by language, specialty, or availability
- Read therapist bios and credentials

**Step 3: Book a Session**
- Select an available time slot that works for you
- Choose your session length (30-120 minutes)
- Review the total cost

**Step 4: Complete Payment**
- Enter your payment information securely
- Receive instant booking confirmation
- Get email confirmation with session details

**Step 5: Communicate Before Your Session**
- Message your therapist to introduce yourself
- Discuss what you'd like to focus on
- Ask any questions about the upcoming session

**Step 6: Join Your Video Session**
- At the scheduled time, click "Join Video Session"
- Allow camera and microphone permissions
- Start your therapy session

**Step 7: Continue Your Journey**
- Rebook with the same therapist for follow-up sessions
- Track your progress over time
- Access session history and notes

**Step 1: Professional Registration**

**Step 2: Set Your Availability**

- Message patients before sessions

**Step 6: Document & Follow Up**
- Add private session notes after appointments
- Send follow-up messages to patients
- Schedule next sessions

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is this platform suitable for crisis situations?**
A: No, this platform is for non-emergency therapy sessions. In a crisis, please call 911 or the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 988.

**Q: Are therapists licensed?**
A: Yes, all therapists undergo credential verification before being approved on the platform.

**Q: Can I choose my own therapist?**
A: Absolutely! Browse profiles, read bios, and select the therapist that's the best fit for you.

**Q: What if I need to cancel or reschedule?**
A: You can cancel or request to reschedule sessions. Cancellation policies are determined by individual therapists.

**Q: Is my information private and secure?**
A: Yes, all communications are encrypted and we follow strict privacy and security protocols.

### Payment Questions

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor.

**Q: When do I pay for a session?**
A: Payment is processed when you book a session, before it takes place.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies vary by therapist. Contact your therapist or support for refund requests.

**Q: Do you accept insurance?**
A: Currently, the platform does not directly bill insurance. However, we can provide receipts you may submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What devices can I use?**
A: The platform works on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones with a modern web browser.

**Q: Do I need to download any software?**
A: No, everything works directly in your web browser. No downloads required.

**Q: What internet speed do I need?**
A: We recommend at least 5 Mbps for optimal video quality.

**Q: What if I have technical issues during a session?**
A: Contact support immediately, and we'll help troubleshoot. Sessions can be rescheduled if technical issues prevent completion.

### For Therapists

**Q: How do I receive payment?**
A: Payments are processed through the platform and transferred to your account according to the payment schedule.

**Q: Can I set my own rates?**
A: Currently, the platform uses standardized pricing. Custom pricing options may be available for enterprise partnerships.

**Q: How do I verify my credentials?**
A: During registration, you'll submit your license information, which our team will verify.

**Q: Can I block out vacation time?**
A: Yes, you have complete control over your availability calendar.
